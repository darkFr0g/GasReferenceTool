Work Area Protection — Quick Reference
Setup Sequence
1
Wear PPE — traffic vest, hard hat
2
Activate dome light / flashers / arrow boards
3
Position vehicle UPSTREAM from work site
4
Flaggers to warn traffic (when necessary)
5
Place advance warning sign(s) FIRST
First device placed
6
Lay down cones / stanchions toward work site
7
Channel traffic using cones
8
Establish work site with barricades
9
Establish downstream termination with cones
10
Set up pedestrian channeling if needed
Minimum 5 ft wide
Demobilization Sequence
1
Wear PPE
2
Clean up work area
3
Break down work site
4
Pick up devices in REVERSE order
5
Work back toward advance warning sign
6
Hazard lights on while leaving lanes
7
Wait for red light before pulling out
8
All road plates pinned and ramped
9
Inspect — no tools / equipment / debris left
